
Reading Checklist: Key Sections from Large Papers & Books
=========================================================

1. Duda et al. – Pattern Classification (2nd Edition)
-----------------------------------------------------
Use for: GDA & Naive Bayes (CS229)
Read:
- Chapter 2: Bayes Decision Theory (Sections 2.1–2.6)
- Chapter 3: Parametric Techniques (Sections 3.1–3.4)
(~40 pages)

2. Vapnik – The Nature of Statistical Learning Theory
-----------------------------------------------------
Use for: ERM, SRM, bias-variance (CS229)
Read:
- Chapter 1: Overview
- Chapter 3: ERM and SRM principles, VC dimension
(~30–40 pages)

3. Rumelhart et al. – Learning Representations by Backpropagation
------------------------------------------------------------------
Use for: Neural Networks and Backpropagation (CS229, DL)
Read:
- Intro + core section on backpropagation derivation
- Skip neuroscience background
(~10–15 pages)

4. Glorot & Bengio – Difficulty of Training Deep Nets
-----------------------------------------------------
Use for: DL initialization (PadhAI)
Read:
- Entire paper (~10 pages)
- Focus on weight initialization and vanishing gradients

5. Vaswani et al. – Attention Is All You Need
---------------------------------------------
Use for: Transformers (LLM module)
Read:
- Section 3: Model Architecture
- Section 4: Positional Encoding
- Section 5: Training setup (skim)
(~10–12 pages)

6. Devlin et al. – BERT: Pre-training of Deep Transformers
----------------------------------------------------------
Use for: LLM Fine-tuning (Hugging Face)
Read:
- Abstract + Intro
- Section 2: Architecture
- Section 3: Pre-training Tasks (MLM, NSP)
- Section 4: Fine-tuning Tasks
(~8–10 pages)

7. Hu et al. – LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation
----------------------------------------
Use for: QLoRA and efficient fine-tuning
Read:
- Sections 2–4 (LoRA method, math, efficiency)
(~5–6 pages)

8. Lewis et al. – Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
------------------------------------------------------
Use for: RAG implementation
Read:
- Introduction
- Section 2: Architecture (retriever + generator)
- Section 3: Retriever/Generator flow
(~6–8 pages)
